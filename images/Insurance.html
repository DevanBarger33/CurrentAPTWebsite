Insurance Questions

How much will it cost? Will insurance cover this?

Costs are different for different patients and different treatments. After your initial evaluation, your physical therapist will be able to estimate the usual charges and average length of treatment for a similar condition. You may ask to see a list of charges and ask for an explanation of how charges are billed. Our office staff is available to discuss charges, payment options, and insurance reimbursements.Please bring a copy of your insurance card, and we will help file your insurance claim.

Do you file insurance claims with insurance providers?

Yes, we send completed claim forms to providers for our patients. Patients are still responsible for their accounts and for paying any deductibles or co-pays. 

Currently, Aptitude Physical Therapy is in network with:

  • Medicare
  • Anthem
  • Assurant Health--HealthLink
  • Blue Cross/Blue Shield
  • Celtic
  • Coventry
  • Group Health Plans
  • HealthLink
  • MultiPlan
  • Rockport
  • Tricare
  • Triwest
  • United Health Care
  • ValuePoint

We send claim forms to out-of-network providers. Our office staff is experienced at dealing with insurance companies and would be glad to help you sort through your insurance policy details.

Do you accept liens? Do you accept accident cases?

We do treat patients who have been in accidents, and we have accepted cases on a lien basis. Please discuss your case with our Office Manager to see what arrangements can be made.

What if I don't have insurance?

If you don't have insurance coverage, please talk to our office staff about financial arrangements to make physical therapy affordable.

What are your privacy policies?

We keep your health information private under Federal HIPAA laws. All your private information is kept confidential in our office. You will be given information about these privacy laws at your first visit. Our office takes care to maintain your privacy and to help you understand the law.
