About Us

Our Mission Statement

At Aptitude Physical Therapy, LLC, we communicate thoroughly with our patients, their caretakers, and their providers. We assist our patients in solving their movement-related issues by developing customized treatment plans. We teach patients what their responsibilities are to improve their quality of life. We strive for successful outcomes and satisfied patients.

What We Do

At Aptitude Physical Therapy, we take time to teach our patients and answer questions about each patient's personal diagnosis and customized treatment plan. We keep current on relevant research and are committed to solving our patients' pain problems.

We Listen

Aptitude Physical Therapy in Springfield, Missouri, provides patient-centered treatment that begins with listening to what the patient has to say. After listening, we take the time to ask and answer questions to fully understand the patient's needs.

We Solve

At Aptitude Physical Therapy, physical therapists work as problem solvers for their patients. Since every patient and injury is unique, our physical therapists will take the time to analyze the patient's symptoms and customize a treatment plan.

We Teach

Our physical therapists share a commitment to continuing education and supporting research. Learning and then teaching is a lifestyle. They take time to teach patients about their personal diagnosis and customized treatment plans.
