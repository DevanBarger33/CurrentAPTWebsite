Student Information & Forms

HS/Undergrad Student Information

Limited slots are available to undergraduate and high school students during summer office hours for shadowing. Students must contact the Aptitude Physical Therapy Office Manager to set up shadowing hours and fill out necessary paperwork. Students should have a working knowledge of HIPAA requirements. CPR/AED certification is recommended before working with patients in any setting. Professional conduct is expected from students at all times. Students are expected to show up on time in professional attire. If a student is unable to attend a scheduled clinical affiliation, the student is expected to contact the Office Manager at 417-890-7787.

PT Student Information

PT students must contact the Aptitude Physical Therapy Office Manager before their clinical affiliation to verify hours. PT students are responsible for completing all necessary documents. Professional conduct is expected from students at all times. Students are expected to show up on time in professional attire. If a student is unable to attend a scheduled clinical affiliation, the student is expected to contact the Office Manager at 417-890-7787.
