Appointment Questions

Can I choose my physical therapist or does my doctor choose one?

Your doctor may refer you to our outpatient clinic, but you are allowed to choose your physical therapist. Missouri state law requires a doctor's orders for physical therapy, and many insurance providers require a referral. Ask us if you need more information about obtaining a referral.

How long will the appointment last, and what will the PT do?

The average appointment time is 45 minutes. Initial evaluations are usually longer because more time is needed for questions and answers. After that evaluation, your physical therapist will explain the treatment plan and what to expect at future visits.
